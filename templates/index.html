CrossFit

CrossFit beckons individuals of all ages and abilities with its inclusive approach to fitness. It merges functional body-weight and weighted exercises into exhilarating, high-tempo workouts. From pull-ups and push-ups to running, weightlifting, gymnastics, and rowing, CrossFit offers a diverse range of challenges. It's a promise of strength, cardiovascular prowess, and enduring stamina, all while shunning monotony.

FastFit ushers in a new era of efficient workouts tailored for the time-conscious individual. With its emphasis on quick, effective exercises, FastFit maximizes results in minimal time. It's the ultimate solution for those seeking to optimize their fitness journey without sacrificing productivity.

CrossFit Kids teaches foundational movement patterns, improving muscular fitness, using a broad and general approach, learning movement patterns for the squat, deadlift, press, and more, through age-appropriate progressions and games with their own body weight or equipment that is easily managed by the age group.

Weightlifting

SMAI Lifting Platforms form the solid base of our weightlifting area. It includes all the essentials such as premium Eleiko and Gymway Calibrated barbells and plates, Half Power Cage, Full Power Cage, Squat Racks, Jerk Blocks, Full Size 4m x 4m Competition Platform, Reverse Hyper, GHD Machine, Belt-Squat and much more. This area is sure to be a meeting place for the professional and novice alike.

Weightlifting showcases the precision and power of strength sports, drawing enthusiasts into its realm of technical mastery and explosive energy. With lifts like the snatch and clean and jerk, it's a pursuit that demands focus, discipline, and dedication, promising not just physical strength but also mental fortitude.

Powerlifting

Strongmen
